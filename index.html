Byeongju Woo

I am a first lieutenant in the Republic of Korea Air Force (ROKAF), currently serving as a research officer at the Agency for Defense Development (ADD), the Korean counterpart to the U.S. DARPA. I've worked on the robust visual recognition in adverse conditions, and Out-of-Distribution generalization.

Previously, I completed my bachelor's degree (Summa Cum Laude) in Computer Science and Engineering at POSTECH.
